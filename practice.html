Practice Guidelines & Ethics

Legal & Ethical Boundaries

Always practice on systems you own or have explicit permission to test. Never test on systems without authorization.

Isolated Environments

Use virtual machines and isolated networks to prevent accidental damage to production systems.

Document Your Learning

Keep detailed notes of techniques learned, tools used, and results obtained for future reference.
